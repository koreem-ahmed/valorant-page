About Valorant

What is Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play, team-based, first-person tactical shooter developed by Riot Games. It's a 5v5 competitive game where players choose from a roster of unique "Agents" with different abilities to outwit and outplay their opponents. The game focuses on strategic gunplay, tactical abilities, and teamwork, with players battling across various maps with the goal of winning rounds by planting or defusing a "spike" or eliminating the opposing team.
